Curated collections of a creative mind.

Welcome to a tactile scrapbook documenting intentional pastimes, daily rhythms, dark literature, and fine artistic expression.

01 / The Unpolished Melody

Moving freely to raw beats allows the fast world to completely slow down. As for singing? My voice misses the register completely—persistently out of key and beautifully bad. Yet, I sing out loud anyway. It is an uncalculated cinematic release of pure joy.

02 / Archival Realism

Sinking into historical film prints, ecological studies, and hidden testimonies. Documentaries act as a beautiful, high-contrast lens looking into micro-realities, allowing us to absorb fragments of the real world at a slower pace.

03 / Dark Prose & Ink Stains

Stepping away from reality into the addictive, high-stakes romance universes written by Ana Huang. The intense devotion, sharp character choices, and dark atmosphere running through the Twisted series offer a complete escape. When the world is still, I channel those emotions back onto paper.

"Still waters hold the changing sky,
Lines of graphite stretch and fade,
We build our walls where stories lie,
Safe within the worlds we made."

04 / Continuous Line Doodles

Express raw thoughts or track down a loose, simple contour of a lily blossom onto our organic workshop paper space below.

05 / Subsurface Stillness

Sinking down underneath the water level where everyday sound completely vanishes. Swimming acts as a fluid meditation: uniform weights, rhythmic strokes, and deep, singular breathing patterns away from the noise of the land.
